Atlantic blue, smacks frothy kisses upon the sand
Grit of sea-salt amidst toes; I sit upon the sand

Briny wind curling softly around the coastal land
Tangle of tresses dry the tears that fall upon the sand

Memories that have bathed along the vanishing strand
Set desires to kneel lonely upon the sand

Cry of gulls in arc over the infinity band
Sound in tandem with my whispered prayers upon the sand

Sea-spray stings in rising, but feet are willing to stand
Stepping with a victorious imprint upon the sand
